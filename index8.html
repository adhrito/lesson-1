Facts about football.

Football, often called association football or soccer, is a team sport played globally where two teams of 11 players try to score by kicking or heading a ball into the opponent's goal. The team with the most goals wins. It's the world's most popular ball game in terms of both participants and spectators.

Football is a popular team sport where two teams compete to score goals by maneuvering a ball into the opponent's net, with the team scoring the most goals being declared the winner.
